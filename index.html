Mount Bromo is one of the active volcanoes in East Java. This mountain has a height of about 2,329 meters above sea level. Mount Bromo is located in four districts: Probolinggo Regency, Pasuruan Regency, Lumajang Regency, and Malang Regency. Mount Bromo is included in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park area. Mount Bromo is famous as a significant tourist attraction in East Java. As a tourist attraction, Bromo is interesting because of its status as an active volcano.

FACTS ABOUT BROMO MOUNT

  • Mount Bromo comes from the Sanskrit Brahma, one of the Main Gods in Hinduism, in the Tengger language. It is spelt Brahma.
  • Mount Bromo is in the same area as Mount Semeru, which is the highest mountain on the island of Java; besides that, there are also Mount Batok, Mount Widodaren, Mount Watangan, and Mount Kursi, which are in the Tengger Mountains complex.
  • This mountain is considered sacred, especially by the people of the Tengger Tribe, who live around the slopes of the mountain. Traditional ceremonies are often held around Mount Bromo every year, such as the Yadnya Kasada (Kasodho).
